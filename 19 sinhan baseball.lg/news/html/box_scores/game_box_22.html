Tigers Subdue SK, 3-1
In a low-scoring tilt between the KIA Tigers and the SK Wyverns, Hyeon-jong Yang was the difference-maker. The KIA left-hander earned player of the game honors in the 3-1 win. Yang got the win, improving to 1-0. He allowed 5 hits and walked 2 over 6 innings. Yang allowed 1 earned run. Se-hyun Kim earned his 2nd save in 2 tries.

Roger Bernadina had a clutch at-bat with one out in the bottom of the sixth for the Tigers. Coming up with a runner on 1st and KIA trailing 1-0, he hit a 2-run home run.

"You can't get too high when things go well, or too low when things go bad. Right now, I'm just happy we got the win," Bernadina told the KIA Daily.

KIA has a mark of 2-2 after the win.
