Song Helps Eagles Run Win Streak to 6
The Hanwha Eagles beat the Samsung Lions, 7-2, at Hanbat Baseball Stadium. Hanwha third baseman Kwang-min Song earned player of the game honors with an outstanding performance. He was 3-4 with a home run and 2 singles. He drove in 4 runs and scored once. Eun-beom Song got the win, improving to 1-0. He worked 6 innings, giving up 2 runs on 6 hits, while striking out 5 and walking 1. Soo-chang Shim finished to wrap up his 2nd save.

Song had a big at-bat with runners on 1st and 2nd in the bottom of the third inning, when he hit a 3-run home run. The Eagles went on to win.

"We looked pretty good up and down," said Hanwha manager Yong-deok Han.

The Eagles, now 6-0, have stormed out of the gates.
