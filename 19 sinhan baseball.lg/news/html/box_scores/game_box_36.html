Wyverns Bang Out 7-3 Win Versus Nexen
SK played host to Nexen at the friendly confines of Munhak Baseball Stadium.

The SK Wyverns knocked off the Nexen Heroes, 7-3, at Munhak Baseball Stadium. Jeong Choi, who captured player of the game honors, was a big reason SK won. The third baseman was 3 for 4 with 2 home runs. Angel L. Sanchez got the win, improving to 1-0. He pitched 5 innings, giving up 10 hits and 2 runs, while striking out 3 and walking 1.

Cheongju, South Korea-born Soo-kwang Ro came through in the clutch. In the bottom of the fifth, with runners on 1st and 3rd and the Wyverns trailing 2-1, he hit a 3-run home run to left to put SK on top.

"If we play like this, we can win a lot of games," Choi told the SK Times.

The win takes SK to 4-3.
