The NC Dinos and Lotte Giants put on quite a show at Sajik Baseball Stadium.

The NC Dinos bested the Lotte Giants, 8-4, at Sajik Baseball Stadium. Hee-dong Kwon was named player of the game. He went 2-5 with a home run and a double. He scored 2 runs and drove in 5. NC reliever Jong-hyun Won picked up the win, bumping his record to 1-0. Seung-lak Son was collared with the loss.

Kwon had a game-changing double in the top of the tenth. On a full count slider from Seung-lak Son, he banged a bases-clearing double to left, giving the Dinos a 7-4 lead. It was the 1st double of the year for Kwon.

"We put it together today and came out with the win," Kwon said in postgame remarks. "That's the main thing."

With the loss, Lotte is 3-4.
