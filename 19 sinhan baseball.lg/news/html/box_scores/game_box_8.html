Kwon 4-6, Dinos Move to 1-0
The NC Dinos put one in the win column with an opening day 17-5 triumph over the LG Twins. The crowd at Masan Baseball Stadium watched Hee-dong Kwon put on a fine performance, for which he was named player of the game. NC reliever Hyun-sik Jang picked up the win, upping his record to 1-0. Woo-chan Cha took the loss.

Kwon had a big at-bat with runners on 1st and 2nd in the bottom of the eighth inning, when he hit a 3-run home run. The Dinos went on to win.

"The name of the game is scoring runs," Kwon said. "We did a good job of that today."
