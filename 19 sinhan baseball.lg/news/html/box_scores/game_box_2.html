Eagles Clip Heroes in Tight Contest
Fans brought energy and enthusiasm to the Nexen Heroes home opener. Sang-woo Cho started for the home team against Jason Wheeler for the Hanwha Eagles. The Heroes were unable to reward the home crowd with a win. The final score was 7-6, Hanwha. Michael Choice was named player of the game. This game was not decided until late. With one out in the top of the ninth, Jin-haeng Choi hit a sacrifice fly. The Eagles took the lead, 7-6.

"Honestly, I'm not frustrated," said Nexen manager ORA TREE. "The effort was great."
