Dinos Get Past LG for 8-7 Win
NC beat LG at Masan Baseball Stadium, eking out a 8-7 win. The win was due in large part to first baseman Xavier Scruggs, who earned player of the game honors. The winner was Chang-min Lim in relief. His record improves to 1-0. Jung-woo Lim took the loss. The Dinos raised their record to 2-0.

Scruggs had a game-changing home run in the bottom of the ninth. On a 1-0 splitter from Jung-woo Lim, he banged a 2-run home run to left, giving the Dinos a 8-7 lead. It was the 1st home run of the year for Scruggs.

"We'll grab a cold one tonight and go after another win tomorrow," Scruggs said.
