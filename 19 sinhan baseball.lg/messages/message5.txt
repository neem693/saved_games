Tae-kyun Kim Slims Up, Turns Heads
All eyes are on <Tae-kyun Kim:player#897> this spring since he came to camp losing an estimated 30 pounds during the off-season.

"I decided that if I'm going to take the next step in my career, I'm going to need to be proactive about it. I figured that losing some weight would help."

Teammates and fans alike are excited to see what's next for Kim during the season.

"I'm determined to be a major part of this organization and bring a championship to the fans."
