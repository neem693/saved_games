Young-suk Jang to Miss Remainder of Season After Bat Swinging Injury
<Young-suk Jang:player#651> will miss the remainder of the season after tearing his Latissimus dorsi muscle swinging at a pitch during the game. 'I felt terrible pain as I swung at the pitch and sure enough, I tore it.' said Jang. An MRI confirmed the diagnosis. Jang will miss the remainder of the year as a result.
