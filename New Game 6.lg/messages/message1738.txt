Dickson Wins June's Rookie Award
The Pacific League made the official announcement today. The Rookie of the Month honor went to <O'Koyea Dickson:player#31771> of <Tohoku Rakuten:team#415>. He was singled out as the top-rated rookie for the month of June.

Last month, the 28-year-old Golden Eagles left fielder played in 22 games and hit .315 with 10 doubles, no triples and 6 home runs. <Dickson:player#31771> also scored 20 times and drove in 24 runs.

For the season <Dickson:player#31771> has produced 72 hits in 289 at-bats for a .249 average with 13 home runs and gotten on base at a .326 clip.
