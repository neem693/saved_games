Moya Wins WL Player of the Week Honors
Western League pitchers tried and tried all week long to stop <Steven Moya:player#30509>, but to no avail.

His eyes would light up when he came to the plate and he hit everything in sight, batting .565 for the week to win the WL Player of the Week Award. The <Chunichi (Ni-Gun):team#429> right fielder cracked 13 hits in 23 at-bats, 1 home run, drove in 6 runs and scored 2 times.

So far in 2018 Moya is batting .372 with 20 home runs, 52 RBIs and 40 runs scored. He has 77 hits in 207 at-bats and a .425 OBP.
