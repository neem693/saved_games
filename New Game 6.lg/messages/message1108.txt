Lamigo's Pena Snags May Batting Title
<Wily Mo Pena:player#36794> may be getting older, but he proved in May that he's still got it.

The <Lamigo Monkeys:team#463> left fielder's strong month has seen him named Chinese Professional Baseball League Batter of the Month.

<Pena:player#36794> batted .293, hit 7 home runs, drove in 19 runs, scored 19 times and compiled a .376 on-base percentage. 

This season <Pena:player#36794> is batting .311 with 48 RBIs, 40 runs scored and 16 home runs.
