Scavuzzo Wins CAL Batter of the Month
For a rookie just breaking in, <Jacob Scavuzzo:player#33253> certainly put together a successful month. It was good enough to win the CAL Batter of the Month award for April.

The 24-year-old left fielder for the <Rancho Cucamonga Quakes:team#226> hit .319 with 7 home runs, 21 RBIs and 14 runs scored.
