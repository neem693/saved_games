Season Done for Tropeano
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the <Salt Lake Bees:team#181>: starting pitcher <Nick Tropeano:player#31818> is out for the year. Tropeano suffered a torn flexor tendon in his elbow on 04/20/2018. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.
