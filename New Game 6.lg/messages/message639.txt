Harper Wins Best Bat Award in April
When <Bryce Harper:player#30240> stepped up to the plate in April he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the National League Batter of the Month award.

The young <Washington:team#60> right fielder posted a .330 batting average with 7 home runs, 20 RBIs and scored 20 runs. <Harper:player#30240> also worked pitchers for 19 walks to post a .440 on-base percentage.
