Watson Is June's Best SAL Hurler
There's something about a young rookie winning the South Atlantic League Pitcher of the Month trophy that sends shock waves through the league. Such is the case with <Nolan Watson:player#24548>, a 21-year-old starter for the <Lexington Legends:team#266>.

In June, he topped off the month with a 4-0 mark and 0.66 ERA in 5 starts, tossed 27.1 innings, fanned 21 and held opposing teams to a .194 average.

In 11 starts this season <Watson:player#24548> has notched a 5-0 record and a 1.84 ERA.
