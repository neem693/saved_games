Rumble Ponies Reliever Cops Pitching Award
Any time a rookie makes a splash on the scene, it's got to be great for his career potential. Just ask <Jamie Callahan:player#33348>, who put together a stellar month out of the pen. The <Binghamton Rumble Ponies:team#187> closer, 23, kept the opposition off balance all month as he held them to a .163 average and walked away with the Eastern League Pitcher of the Month award for June.

Callahan posted a 2-1 mark and a 2.08 ERA in 13 relief appearances. In 13 innings, he had 5 saves, 17 strikeouts, and 5 walks.

<Callahan:player#33348> has collected 18 saves for the year with 49 strikeouts and 18 walks in 37 innings while registering a 1.70 ERA and a 3-3 won-lost record.
