Week's Top Performer Honored by CAL
So far in his young career, <Luis Barrera:player#33387> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the California League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 22-year-old <Stockton Ports:team#220> left fielder hit at a torrid .476 clip as he collected 10 hits in 21 at-bats, 3 home runs, 7 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

In 23 games this year Barrera has posted a .271 average and put up these numbers: 4 home runs, 10 RBIs and 8 runs scored.
