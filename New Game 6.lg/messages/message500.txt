<Casey Hughston:player#578> kept his eye on the ball last week and now all eyes are on him. The <Bradenton Marauders:team#228> center fielder easily swung his way to the Florida State League Player of the Week Award with 11 hits in 26 at-bats.

Hughston hit .423 last week with 2 home runs, 12 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

Thus far this year <Hughston:player#578> has hit .314 with 2 home runs and 16 RBIs.
