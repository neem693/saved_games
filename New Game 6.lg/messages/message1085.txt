TEX Announces Outstanding Batter Award
<Yordan Alvarez:player#41803> made his presence known as one of the best young stars in the Texas League this past month.

The <Corpus Christi Hooks:team#216> right fielder shined through the month of May and has won the Batter of the Month award.

The 20-year-old hit .317 with 8 home runs, 21 RBIs and 20 runs scored.

Alvarez is hitting .303 this season with 10 home runs, 34 RBIs and 31 runs scored. He has 61 hits in 201 at-bats.
