Robbins Wins SL Player of the Week Honors
When it came to hitting the baseball, <Mason Robbins:player#169> had few peers in the Southern League this past week.

The talented right fielder for Birmingham tagged opposing pitchers for a .429 average and was voted the Player of the Week Award. Included in his stats were 9 hits in 21 at-bats, 3 homers, 5 runs scored and 7 RBIs.

<Robbins:player#169> is currently batting .342 with 6 home runs and 20 RBIs.
