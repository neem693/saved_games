Bolt Wins CAL Player of the Week
So far in his young career, <Skye Bolt:player#32861> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the California League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 24-year-old <Stockton Ports:team#220> right fielder hit at a torrid .391 clip as he collected 9 hits in 23 at-bats, 2 home runs, 7 RBIs and 6 runs scored.
