Estevez Punishes CAL Pitchers This Week
So far in his young career, <Omar Estevez:player#43047> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the California League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 20-year-old <Rancho Cucamonga Quakes:team#226> shortstop hit at a torrid .560 clip as he collected 14 hits in 25 at-bats, no home runs, 4 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

This season <Estevez:player#43047> has no home runs and a .410 batting average.
