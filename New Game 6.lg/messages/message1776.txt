EL Player of the Week Chosen
This week he took his hitting to another level. That's why <Rashad Crawford:player#33105> of the <Trenton Thunder:team#192> was named the Eastern League Player of the Week.

For the week the 24-year-old left fielder batted .500 (14-for-28) with no home runs, 4 runs and 3 RBIs.

Thus far this year Crawford is hitting .292 with 1 home run, 23 RBIs and 14 runs scored.
