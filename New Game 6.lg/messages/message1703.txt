Donaldson Takes Best Bat Trophy in June
Free agent-to-be <Josh Donaldson:player#24862> has inflated his asking price over the past 30 days by winning the American League Batter of the Month.

In June, the 32-year-old <Toronto:team#59> third baseman hit .327 with 11 home runs and 23 RBIs.

If he keeps putting numbers like that up, he could be hauling in a big wad of cash at this time next year.

This season <Donaldson:player#24862> has hit at a .296 clip with 54 RBIs, 24 home runs and 60 runs scored. He has played in 81 games.
