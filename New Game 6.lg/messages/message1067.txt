Pitcher of the Month Named in AL
Veteran <Chris Sale:player#30245> put together quite a successful stretch in May for the <Boston Red Sox:team#34>, earning himself the American League Pitcher of the Month award.

Sale racked up a 5-1 record in 6 starts, posting a 1.96 ERA over 41.1 innings and striking out 48.

This season Sale has crafted a 8-3 mark with a 2.75 ERA in 78.2 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .234 against him and he has 84 strikeouts.
