TEX Top Batter Trophy Won by Diaz
At only 21 years of age, <Yusniel Diaz:player#25569> could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the <Tulsa Drillers:team#211>, as June was one to remember for the center fielder and he has captured the Texas League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .380 with 5 home runs, 15 RBIs and 17 runs scored.

To date this year <Diaz:player#25569> is batting .380 with 5 home runs.
