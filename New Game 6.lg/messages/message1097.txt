Pacific League Names Rookie of Month
<Hisanori Yasuda:player#46059>'s eyes seem to light up whenever he sees the baseball. The young <Chiba Lotte:team#411> third baseman has been hitting everything in sight for the better part of a month.

In May he was voted the Rookie of the Month Award.

During the month he hit .297 with 4 doubles, 2 triples and 4 home runs. <Yasuda:player#46059> also scored 11 times and drove in 15 runs.

Yasuda is batting .295 this season in 51 games with 10 home runs, 29 RBIs and 28 runs scored. He has 64 hits in 217 at-bats.
