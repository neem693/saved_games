Jose Celestino Lopez Wins JCL Trophy
A battle-tested <Jose Celestino Lopez:player#36799> declared war on opposing pitchers last week, attacking the ball like the well-schooled veteran he is. Today the 34-year-old <Yokohama DeNA:team#421> first baseman collected the Central League Player of the Week Award.

He wrapped up the week with a .538 average, 14 hits in 26 at-bats, 2 home runs, 8 RBIs and 8 runs scored.

Thus far this season Lopez is hitting .299 with 11 home runs, 39 RBIs and 33 runs scored. He has 70 hits in 234 at-bats and has played in 57 games.
