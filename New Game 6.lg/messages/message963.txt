MLB News: The All-Star Game Voting Begins
"Vote Early and Vote Often"

While that may not be true for elections, that's the message coming out of the Major League Baseball offices for the upcoming All-Star game, scheduled for Tuesday, July 17th, 2018. The ballots have been released, and everyone can <cast your ballot:page#41> up until a few days before the game, when the final rosters will be released.
