Uni-President Starter Wins June's Pitcher Award 
<Wen-Yang Liao:player#39851> was a long shot to make the <Uni-President 7-Eleven Lions:team#462> this year, especially being a 30-year-old rookie. Now the <Uni-President:team#462> hurler has parlayed a 2-0 record into the Chinese Professional Baseball League Pitcher of the Month award.

In June, he chalked up 16 strikeouts in 19 innings and compiled a 1.42 ERA.

Thus far this season <Liao:player#39851> has posted a 4-2 record, started 12 games, struck out 54 batters in 60.2 innings and compiled a 5.49 ERA.
