EL Player of the Week Chosen
So far in his young career, <Koji Yamakawa:player#42927> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Eastern League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 21-year-old <Tokyo Yakult (Ni-Gun) Swallows:team#434> catcher hit at a torrid .526 clip as he collected 10 hits in 19 at-bats, 2 home runs, 3 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

So far this season <Yamakawa:player#42927> is batting .284 with 4 home runs.
