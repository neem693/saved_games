Injury Sidelines Perez for Season
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the <Elizabethton Twins:team#306>: reliever <Bryant Perez:player#46007> is out for the year. Perez suffered a stretched elbow ligament on 06/30/2018. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.
