Reliever Dolis Top JCL Pitcher in April 
<Rafael Dolis:player#29859> was the anchor in the <Hanshin:team#418> bullpen last month and snatched up the Central League Pitcher of the Month honors for April. The 30-year-old is in the prime of his career and pitching like he intends to stay around baseball for quite some time to come.

He threw 15.2 innings over 14 relief appearances and collected a 3-1 record with 20 strikeouts and 9 saves while etching his 2.87 ERA.
