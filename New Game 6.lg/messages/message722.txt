Players Suspended in Marines-Swallows Donnybrook
<Tony Blanco:player#36817> of the <Chiba Lotte (Ni-Gun) Marines:team#423> and <Masanori Ishikawa:player#36984> of the <Tokyo Yakult (Ni-Gun) Swallows:team#434> received notification from the league commissioner's office that they had been suspended for 3 and 7 games respectively for their part in the on-field ruckus in a recent game at Urawa Baseball Ground. 

Blanco is batting .316 this year with no home runs, 3 RBIs and 4 runs scored. He has 6 hits in 19 at-bats.
