Jung Selected Top Player
<Dong-wook Jung:player#45936> of the Hwaseong Heroes had an outstanding week and today was voted the Player of the Week by the Korean Futures League.

For the week the 22-year-old catcher batted .583 (7-for-12) with 2 home runs and 8 RBIs.

This season <Jung:player#45936> is batting .241 with 2 home runs. He has scored 7 runs and driven in 11.
