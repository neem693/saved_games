Abe Launches Number 400
"I wasn't trying to hit a homer. I just wanted to make contact."

That's what <Shinnosuke Abe:player#37322> told reporters after he made baseball history today at Tokyo Dome. The prolific slugger belted home run #400 in the <Yomiuri Giants:team#422> 12-2 victory over the <Tohoku Rakuten Golden Eagles:team#415>.

The standout first baseman collected 2 hits in 4 at-bats with 2 RBI and 1 run scored.

"I guess this puts me in baseball's elite," said Abe. "I don't feel any different. I probably should, but I don't. Still, it is a nice achievement. I did get a little excited, didn't I?"

During his career <Abe:player#37322> is batting .285 and has totaled 400 home runs.
