Lambert Earns CAL's Best Pitcher Honor
Over the past month <Peter Lambert:player#27162> has turned quite a few heads. He's young, talented and able to pitch with the established arms in the California League. He proved all that and now he's been named May's Pitcher of the Month.

The rookie for the <Lancaster JetHawks:team#233> posted 4-0 mark and a 2.30 ERA. In 6 starts he tossed 31.1 innings, permitted 26 hits and fanned 23 batters.

This season <Lambert:player#27162> has a 5-1 record with a 2.37 ERA.
