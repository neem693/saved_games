Tromp Takes Home EL Top Batter Award
It's perhaps an award he'll win many more times through his career, as at age 24 <Jiandido Tromp:player#33721> has a lot of baseball ahead of him.

But for now the young <Reading Fightin Phils:team#198> star can enjoy the numbers he put up in May, which have garnered him the nod as the Eastern League Batter of the Month.

His breakout month featured a .362 average with 11 home runs, 42 RBIs and 24 runs scored.

So far this year <Tromp:player#33721> is hitting .313 with 16 home runs.
