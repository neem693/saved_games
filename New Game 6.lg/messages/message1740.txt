Brown Takes EL's Best Bat Award
Players often talk about having career years. June was a career month for <Seth Brown:player#690>.

The 25-year-old <Yokohama (Ni-Gun):team#427> left fielder bested all others and has been named the Eastern League Batter of the Month.

He put together an eye-popping month that saw him hit .338, with 6 home runs, 21 RBIs, 13 runs scored and a .466 on-base percentage.

In the 2018 season <Brown:player#690> is hitting .275 with 9 home runs.
