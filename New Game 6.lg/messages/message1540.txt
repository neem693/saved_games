Early Season End for Edgin
In a news release, the <Norfolk Tides:team#165> announced that reliever <Josh Edgin:player#31041> would miss the rest of the season due to a stretched elbow ligament. Edgin sustained the injury on 06/19/2018. He "will be sorely missed," said his manager.

For the year Edgin has saved 5 games while posting an ERA of 1.95 and 32 strikeouts in 32.1 innings. His totals also include a 2-1 record in 31 relief appearances.
