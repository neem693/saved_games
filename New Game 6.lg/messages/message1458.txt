Williams Wins NL Honor
<Nick Williams:player#32776> has the tools to stick in this league for many years. The 24-year-old right fielder of the <Philadelphia Phillies:team#51> had a hot bat in the National League over the past seven days and won the Player of the Week honors.

<Williams:player#32776> batted .438 with 7 hits in 16 at-bats, 3 home runs and 7 RBIs.
