Jung Honored by Korean Futures League
<Dong-wook Jung:player#45936> captured all the headlines last week and today he added to his booty with the Korean Futures League Player of the Week trophy.

The <Hwaseong Heroes:team#455> catcher hammered league hurlers for 9 hits in 17 at-bats, a .529 average and 1 home run. He also collected 8 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

So far this season <Jung:player#45936> is batting .283 with 3 home runs.
