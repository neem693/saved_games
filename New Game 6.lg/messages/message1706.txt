Gray Top NL Starter in June
<Jon Gray:player#34100> took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the National League Pitcher of the Month award for June.

The 26-year-old <Rockies:team#39> starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 4-0 won-lost record in 6 starts. This month he fanned 36 batters in 36.2 innings and compiled a 2.21 ERA.

To date this season, <Gray:player#34100> has a record of 6 wins and 4 losses and a 4.28 ERA. He has appeared in 18 games and struck out 92 in 96.2 innings.
