Season-Ending Injury for Kinneman
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the <Lakewood BlueClaws:team#265>: left fielder <Brett Kinneman:player#26913> is out for the year. Kinneman suffered a torn posterior cruciate ligament on 06/08/2018. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.
