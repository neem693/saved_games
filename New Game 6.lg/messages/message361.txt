Kao Selected Player of the Week
<Kuo-Hui Kao:player#40101> swung a blistering bat last week for <Fubon:team#461>, rapping out 10 hits in 22 at-bats against Guardians opponents to propel himself to the Chinese Professional Baseball League Player of the Week Award.

Whenever the left fielder hit the ball, he hit it with authority. The 32-year-old racked up a .455 batting average with 2 home runs, 8 RBIs and 8 runs scored.
