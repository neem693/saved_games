Baltimore Orioles Owner Peter Angelos Passes Away 
The family of <Baltimore Orioles:team#33> owner Peter Angelos announced today that he had died after a prolonged illness. His replacement is expected to be his son, Luis Angelos, who has served in many capacities with the club. Some sources said the younger Angelos was a "chip off the old block," while others commented that he differed in many respects from his father. Speaking off the record, a Baltimore Orioles official commented, "Luis could be described as controlling in financial matters and lenient in personality traits."
