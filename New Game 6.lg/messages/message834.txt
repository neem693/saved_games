Week's Best Player Honored by KBO
<Jared Hoying:player#30883> did nothing to hurt his value as an upcoming free agent last week, making contact with the ball at every opportunity and reeling in the Korean Baseball Organization Player of the Week Award.

The right fielder hit .370 for the <Eagles:team#440>, recording 10 hits in 27 at-bats, 4 home runs, 9 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

This season <Hoying:player#30883> is batting .335 with 14 home runs. His totals include 49 RBIs and 32 runs scored.
