Great Week for Seibu (Ni-Gun)'s Mauro Gomez
A battle-tested <Mauro Gomez:player#23216> declared war on opposing pitchers last week, attacking the ball like the well-schooled veteran he is. Today the 33-year-old <Seibu (Ni-Gun):team#426> first baseman collected the Eastern League Player of the Week Award.

Thus far in 2018 Gomez is hitting .476 with 2 home runs, 5 RBIs, 6 runs scored and has compiled a .560 OBP.
