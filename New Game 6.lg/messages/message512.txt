EL Player of the Week Goes to Rosario of Hokkaido Nippon Ham (Ni-Gun)
It was announced today that <Hokkaido Nippon Ham (Ni-Gun):team#424> left fielder <Henrry Rosario:player#36076> snagged the Eastern League Player of the Week Award for his outstanding play last week.

He posted a .429 batting average as he collected 9 hits, 3 home runs and 9 RBIs.

In 28 games this season <Rosario:player#36076> has compiled a .284 batting average with 27 hits and 7 home runs. He has also scored 11 times and knocked in 24 runs.
