CPBL's Star Player Named
This week he took his hitting to another level. That's why <Jason Rogers:player#30635> of the <Fubon Guardians:team#461> was named the Chinese Professional Baseball League Player of the Week.

For the week the 30-year-old third baseman batted .500 (9-for-18) with 2 home runs, 5 runs and 11 RBIs.

Hitting .350 to date, Rogers has 9 home runs and 34 RBIs. He has played in 30 games.
