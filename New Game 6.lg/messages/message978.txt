Hampyeong Loses Seo for Season
<Duk-won Seo:player#43821> has to call it a season. The <Hampyeong Tigers:team#458> reliever learned today that the full extent his injury suffered on 05/23/2018 is worse than hoped. He will not play again this year according to club doctors, who diagnosed the injury as a partially torn ulnar collateral ligament and stated that he would not be able to play for 6-7 months.   The team will likely place Seo on the disabled list today.

This year <Seo:player#43821> has notched no saves. He has 16 strikeouts and 9 walks in 18.2 innings while registering a 4.34 ERA and a 1-2 won-lost record.
