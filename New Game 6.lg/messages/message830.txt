Big Bat Pays Off, Ogino Player of the Week
<Takashi Ogino:player#37287> is respected by most <Chiba Lotte Marines:team#411> fans for his no-frills style of play. The 32-year-old center fielder went about his business with vigor last week to grab the Pacific League Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .579 average (11-for-19) with 1 home run, 5 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

This year <Ogino:player#37287> has socked 41 hits in 131 at-bats for a .313 average with 3 home runs. He is getting on base at a .371 pace and his totals also include 15 RBIs and 20 runs scored.
