EL Player of the Week Selected
<Shingo Ishikawa:player#37578> of the Yomiuri (Ni-Gun) Giants had an outstanding week and today was voted the Player of the Week by the Eastern League.

In the 2018 season <Ishikawa:player#37578> is hitting .302 with 5 home runs.
