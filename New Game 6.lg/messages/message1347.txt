PC Names Grizzlies Davis Winner
So far in his young career, <J.D. Davis:player#32> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Pacific Conference in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 25-year-old <Fresno Grizzlies:team#183> third baseman hit at a torrid .333 clip as he collected 8 hits in 24 at-bats, 2 home runs, 5 RBIs and 2 runs scored.

For the year <Davis:player#32> is hitting .290 with 11 home runs. His stats include 31 runs batted in and 28 runs scored.
