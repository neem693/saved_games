KBO Awards Player of the Week Trophy
Veteran <Yong-taik Park:player#38549> had a great week at the plate for the <Twins:team#448> to capture the majority of votes for Player of the Week in the Korean Baseball Organization.

The 39-year-old LG left fielder built a .500 average on the strength of 13 hits in 26 at-bats, 2 home runs and 10 RBIs.

For the season <Park:player#38549> is hitting .358, batted in 43 runs with 9 home runs and 37 runs scored.
