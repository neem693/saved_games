Ogino Leaves Team to Care for Ailing Mother
<Takashi Ogino:player#37287> of the Marines is expected to miss at least the next game. Ogino has taken a leave of absence to be with his ailing mother. "I told him to take all the time he needs," said manager Tsutomu Ito. "I know what he's been going through, and the most important thing is that he's there for both her and the family."
