Golden Eagles Okajima Named JPL's #1 Player
It was a sweet day for <Takero Okajima:player#37239> of the <Tohoku Rakuten Golden Eagles:team#415>, who found out he has been named the Pacific League Player of the Week.

The <Golden Eagles:team#415> right fielder put up a solid .429 average and contributed 3 home runs and 7 RBIs to claim the honors.
