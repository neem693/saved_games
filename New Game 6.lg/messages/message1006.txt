Tsutsugo Honored by Central League
<Yoshitomo Tsutsugo:player#37349> is respected by most <Yokohama DeNA BayStars:team#421> fans for his no-frills style of play. The 26-year-old left fielder went about his business with vigor last week to grab the Central League Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .636 average (14-for-22) with 3 home runs, 7 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

On the year <Tsutsugo:player#37349> is hitting .331 with 55 hits, 14 home runs, 38 RBIs, .423 OBP and has scored 31 runs.
