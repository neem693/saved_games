Red-Hot Rampage Reaps CPBL Reward
Veteran <Yung-Chi Chen:player#40098> had a great week at the plate for the <7-Eleven Lions:team#462> to capture the majority of votes for Player of the Week in the Chinese Professional Baseball League.

The 34-year-old Uni-President third baseman built a .500 average on the strength of 10 hits in 20 at-bats, no home runs and 3 RBIs.

This season <Chen:player#40098> is swinging the bat at a .256 rate with 43 hits in 168 at-bats. To date he has hit 2 home runs, scored 29 runs and driven in 16.
