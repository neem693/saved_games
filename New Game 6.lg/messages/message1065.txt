AL Honors Trout, #1 Batter in May
<Mike Trout:player#29909> battered American League pitchers all May and wound up taking home the Batter of the Month Award.

"I've been around Major League Baseball long enough to know you really have to work hard day in and day out to be able to put up this kind of consistency," the 26-year-old Trout told reporters. "I put in a lot of work, even on the days I might not take the field, and it's always nice when everything comes together for me like it did this last month."

In 28 games, Trout hit .374 with 10 home runs, 29 RBIs and scored 23 runs.

To date, <Trout:player#29909> has collected 61 hits for a .300 average. He has posted 18 home runs, scored 45 runs and driven in 45 runs.
