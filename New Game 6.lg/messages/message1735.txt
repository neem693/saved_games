Yokohama DeNA's Tsutsugo Collects June Batting Honor
<Yoshitomo Tsutsugo:player#37349> battered Central League pitchers all June and wound up taking home the Batter of the Month Award.

"I've been around Nippon Professional Baseball League long enough to know you really have to work hard day in and day out to be able to put up this kind of consistency," the 26-year-old Tsutsugo told reporters. "I put in a lot of work, even on the days I might not take the field, and it's always nice when everything comes together for me like it did this last month."

In 25 games, Tsutsugo hit .344 with 6 home runs, 26 RBIs and scored 19 runs.

So far this season <Tsutsugo:player#37349> has gotten 91 hits in 271 at-bats for a .336 batting average with 23 home runs. His stats include 68 RBIs and 53 runs scored. He has played in 73 games.
