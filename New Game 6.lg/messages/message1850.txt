Injury Brings Season to End for Chiu
In a news release, the <Ogden Raptors:team#339> announced that second baseman <Marcus Chiu:player#25832> would miss the rest of the season due to a broken bone in the elbow. Chiu sustained the injury on 07/01/2018. He "will be sorely missed," said his manager.

So far this year in 15 games Chiu is hitting .283 with no home runs, 5 RBIs and 8 runs scored.
