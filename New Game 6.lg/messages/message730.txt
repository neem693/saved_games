AC Names Best Player
So far in his young career, <Dustin Fowler:player#35605> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the American Conference in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 23-year-old <Nashville Sounds:team#173> center fielder hit at a torrid .375 clip as he collected 12 hits in 32 at-bats, 4 home runs, 7 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

Thus far this season <Fowler:player#35605> has hit .308 with 8 home runs, 15 RBIs and scored 18 times.
