Season-Ending Injury for Gowdy
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the <Williamsport Crosscutters:team#286>: reliever <Kevin Gowdy:player#28298> is out for the year. Gowdy suffered a torn rotator cuff on 06/24/2018. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.
