Westbrook Brings Home SL Batter of Month Honor 
<Jamie Westbrook:player#34653> made his presence known as one of the best young stars in the Southern League this past month.

The <Jackson Generals:team#203> second baseman shined through the month of April and has won the Batter of the Month award.

The 22-year-old hit .376 with 3 home runs, 24 RBIs and 13 runs scored.
