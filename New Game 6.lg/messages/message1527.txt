Season Over for Pena
<Andres Pena:player#45054> has to call it a season. The <Bowling Green Hot Rods:team#273> reliever learned today that the full extent his injury suffered on 06/17/2018 is worse than hoped. He will not play again this year according to club doctors, who diagnosed the injury as a fractured elbow and stated that he would not be able to play for 6 months.   The team will likely place Pena on the disabled list today.
