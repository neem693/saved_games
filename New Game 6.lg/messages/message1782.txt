Benes Honored by New York-Penn League
<Shane Benes:player#1259> went on a week-long batting spree against New York-Penn League pitchers and hauled in the Player of the Week trophy.

The <Auburn:team#281> third baseman battered the opposition for 12 hits in 28 at-bats, 1 home run, 3 RBIs and scored 6 runs. He hit .429 and posted a .467 on-base percentage.

<Benes:player#1259> is batting .333 with 1 home runs and 10 RBIs this season.
