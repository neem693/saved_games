Season Over for Binford
<Christian Binford:player#32319> has to call it a season. The <Bowie Baysox:team#195> reliever learned today that the full extent his injury suffered on 04/29/2018 is worse than hoped. He will not play again this year according to club doctors, who diagnosed the injury as a torn rotator cuff and stated that he would not be able to play for 12 months.   The team will likely place Binford on the disabled list today.
