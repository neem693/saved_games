CAL Presents Player of the Week Trophy
So far in his young career, <D.J. Peters:player#20265> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the California League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 22-year-old <Rancho Cucamonga Quakes:team#226> right fielder hit at a torrid .364 clip as he collected 8 hits in 22 at-bats, 3 home runs, 5 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

So far this season <Peters:player#20265> has put up a .221 batting average with 30 hits, 4 home runs and 12 RBIs.
