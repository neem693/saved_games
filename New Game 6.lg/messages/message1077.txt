Nunez Awarded May's AC Top Bat Prize
<Nashville Sounds:team#173> fans believe they've got a talented young star in <Renato Nunez:player#31194>.

He did nothing to dissuade that notion in May, as he hit .327 with 9 home runs, 20 RBIs and 18 runs scored.

The end result was the third baseman being named the American Conference Batter of the Month.

<Nunez:player#31194> on the season is batting .301 with 12 home runs. His totals include 29 RBIs and 26 runs scored. He has played in 43 games.
