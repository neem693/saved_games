Reds Selman Snags APP Player of the Week
So far in his young career, <Shane Selman:player#24135> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Appalachian League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 21-year-old <Greeneville Reds:team#304> center fielder hit at a torrid .444 clip as he collected 12 hits in 27 at-bats, 3 home runs, 9 RBIs and 9 runs scored.
