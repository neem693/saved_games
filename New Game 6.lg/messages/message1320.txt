Injury Sidelines Havrilak for Season
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the <Daytona Tortugas:team#236>: third baseman <Joey Havrilak:player#23336> is out for the year. Havrilak suffered a torn posterior cruciate ligament on 06/04/2018. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.

Havrilak is batting .236 this season in 30 games with 2 home runs, 12 RBIs and 10 runs scored. He has 17 hits in 72 at-bats.
