Lin Says No Leads in Home Robbery
<Lamigo Monkeys:team#463> reliever <Kuo-Yu Lin:player#41915>'s home in <Lamigo:team#463> was robbed recently and he said thus far local police don't have any suspects or leads.

"They said they are investigating, but nothing to report right now," Lin said. "I can't believe the guy is probably going to get away with it."

Lin said the support of his teammates has been fantastic and they all simply want to move on and focus on baseball.
