Star of the Week Named in CPBL
He was a hard-hitting machine all week long. That's why <Yi-Chuan Lin:player#39213> of the <Fubon Guardians:team#461> was named the Chinese Professional Baseball League Player of the Week.

For the week the 32-year-old first baseman batted .522 (12-for-23) with 3 home runs, 7 runs and 9 RBIs.

Lin is batting .423 this year with 9 home runs, 30 RBIs and 24 runs scored. He has 47 hits in 111 at-bats.
