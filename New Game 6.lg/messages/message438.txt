KFL Awards Player of the Week
Today the Korean Futures League announced the Player of the Week Award. This week's honoree is <Icheon:team#457> first baseman <Dae-young Yoon:player#39086>. When he steps into the batter's box, it's usually bad news for pitchers.

For the week he batted .600 (15-for-25) with 6 home runs and 16 RBIs.
