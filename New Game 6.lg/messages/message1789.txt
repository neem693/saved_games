Kim Wins KBO Best Player Award
This week <Hyun-soo Kim:player#38179>, left fielder for the <LG Twins:team#448>, hit everything in sight to win the Korean Baseball Organization Player of the Week trophy.

He rattled fences and pitchers as he banged out a .542 average and .593 on-base percentage. <Kim:player#38179> logged 13 hits in 24 at-bats, 1 home run, 4 RBIs, 3 walks and scored 3 times.

<Kim:player#38179> is batting at a .353 pace this season, picking up 119 hits in 337 at-bats with 8 home runs, 39 RBIs and 59 runs scored.
