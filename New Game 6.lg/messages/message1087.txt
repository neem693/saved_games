Clearwater's Randolph Top FSL Batter in May 
At only 20 years of age, <Cornelius Randolph:player#27077> could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the <Clearwater Threshers:team#241>, as May was one to remember for the left fielder and he has captured the Florida State League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .315 with 6 home runs, 18 RBIs and 19 runs scored.

This season <Randolph:player#27077> is swinging the bat at a .318 rate with 48 hits in 151 at-bats. To date he has hit 9 home runs, scored 28 runs and driven in 30.
