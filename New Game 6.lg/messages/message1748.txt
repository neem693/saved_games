Best Pitcher Picked by Korean Futures League
<Ju-young Son:player#44341> took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the Korean Futures League Pitcher of the Month award for June.

The 19-year-old <Twins:team#457> starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 3-0 won-lost record in 4 starts. This month he fanned 16 batters in 22.1 innings and compiled a 2.01 ERA.

Up to now <Son:player#44341> has compiled an ERA of 4.45 with a 6-4 mark over 12 starts. He has 38 strikeouts in 56.2 innings and has held opponents to a .329 batting average.
