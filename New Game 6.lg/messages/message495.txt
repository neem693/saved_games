EL Player of the Week, Stewart of Bowie
So far in his young career, <D.J. Stewart:player#21326> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Eastern League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 24-year-old <Bowie Baysox:team#195> left fielder hit at a torrid .500 clip as he collected 14 hits in 28 at-bats, 3 home runs, 11 RBIs and 8 runs scored.

Hitting .419 to date, Stewart has 4 home runs and 21 RBIs. He has played in 17 games.
