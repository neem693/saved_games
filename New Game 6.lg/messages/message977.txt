Nakamura Has Season End
Team officials for the <Hokkaido Nippon-Ham Fighters:team#413> confirmed that bone chips in his elbow will force <Masaru Nakamura:player#37276> to miss the rest of the season. Nakamura sustained the injury on 05/22/2018. It remains to be seen who will replace Nakamura on the active roster.

His record for the year is 5-2 in 9 starts and <Nakamura:player#37276> has a 2.48 ERA.
