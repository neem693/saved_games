Trading Deadline Here
The busiest day of the year for general managers in Chinese Professional Baseball League has arrived - today is the trading deadline.

Each general manager and their staff has been working overtime for days, crunching numbers and reviewing scouting reports in a final attempt to find that one key piece that will change the fate of their franchise.

Will they be able to strike the perfect deal? Time will be the ultimate judge, but that won't stop tomorrow's headlines from declaring winners and losers.
