Chono Earns JCL Player of the Week
A battle-tested <Hisayoshi Chono:player#37313> declared war on opposing pitchers last week, attacking the ball like the well-schooled veteran he is. Today the 33-year-old <Yomiuri:team#422> right fielder collected the Central League Player of the Week Award.

He wrapped up the week with a .400 average, 10 hits in 25 at-bats, 3 home runs, 6 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

For the season <Chono:player#37313> has produced 62 hits in 195 at-bats for a .318 average with 10 home runs and gotten on base at a .395 clip.
