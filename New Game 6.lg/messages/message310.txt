CPBL's Best Player, Kuo of Lamigo
<Lamigo Monkeys:team#463> second baseman <Yen-Wen Kuo:player#39963> rose above the controversy surrounding his soon-to-expire contract, thriving under pressure last week and copping the Player of the Week award in the Chinese Professional Baseball League.

Kuo put up a .474 batting average (9-for-19) with 1 home run, 3 RBIs and scored 5 times.
