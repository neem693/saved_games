Andujar Caps Great Week
So far in his young career, <Miguel Andujar:player#32595> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the International League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 23-year-old <Scranton WB RailRiders:team#161> third baseman hit at a torrid .371 clip as he collected 13 hits in 35 at-bats, 3 home runs, 9 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

To date, <Andujar:player#32595> has collected 71 hits for a .318 average. He has posted 11 home runs, scored 28 runs and driven in 46 runs.
