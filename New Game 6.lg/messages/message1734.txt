JPL Presents Batter of Month Trophy to Dickson
<O'Koyea Dickson:player#31771> is on a tear! After posting a .315 average in June, the <Tohoku Rakuten:team#415> left fielder took home the Pacific League Batter of the Month award handed out earlier today.

He cracked 28 hits with 6 home runs and 24 RBIs and scored 20 runs. The 28-year-old also collected 15 free passes and posted a .419 on-base percentage.

<Dickson:player#31771> is currently hitting at a .249 pace with 13 home runs, 50 RBIs and has scored 44 times.
