Egawa Wins JPL Player of the Week Honors
It was announced today that <Fukuoka SoftBank:team#412> right fielder <Tomoaki Egawa:player#37019> snagged the Pacific League Player of the Week Award for his outstanding play last week.

He posted a .600 batting average as he collected 12 hits, 2 home runs and 15 RBIs.

So far this season <Egawa:player#37019> is batting .340 with 4 home runs.
