Fubon's Starter Receives CPBL Pitching Award 
32-year-olds don't often go 3-0 in and haul away the best pitcher award, but <Bruce Billings:player#25309> did all that. Now, he's the toast of the Chinese Professional Baseball League. Today he was crowned the Pitcher of the Month for April.

In 4 starts he posted a 1.98 ERA and struck out 13 batters in 27.1 innings.
