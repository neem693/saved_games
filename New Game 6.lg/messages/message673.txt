Chunichi (Ni-Gun)'s Duffy Bags WL Top Batter for April
<Matt E. Duffy:player#31827>, the Chunichi (Ni-Gun) first baseman, certainly made a name for himself this past month. He banged out a .394 batting average with 26 hits, 7 home runs, 22 RBIs and scored 19 times.

That kind of hitting earned him the Western League Batter of the Month honors for April.
