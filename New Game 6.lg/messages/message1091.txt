SAL Batter of the Month Honors to Fernandez
<Lexington Legends:team#266> fans may just have witnessed a star player blossoming before their eyes in May.

<Xavier Fernandez:player#35489> put up a remarkable month at the age of 22 and has been named the South Atlantic League Batter of the Month.

For the month, <Fernandez:player#35489> hit .283 with 4 home runs, 16 RBIs and 21 runs scored.

Fernandez is hitting .259 this year with 6 home runs, 22 RBIs and 29 runs scored. He has 44 hits in 170 at-bats, 25 walks and a .348 on-base percentage. <Fernandez:player#35489> has played in 47 games.
