Nakamura Receives April Rookie Award
<Hiroshima Toyo:team#419>'s <Shosei Nakamura:player#46154> collected 14 hits to snare the Central League Rookie of the Month for April.

He batted .264 with 2 home runs, 7 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

The 18-year-old is barely old enough to shave, but he could definitely contend for Rookie of the Year if he keeps up this pace.
