Flying Squirrels Taylor April's Top Hurler
Any time a rookie makes a splash on the scene, it's got to be great for his career potential. Just ask <Cory Taylor:player#21371>, who put together a stellar month out of the pen. The <Richmond Flying Squirrels:team#190> reliever, 24, kept the opposition off balance all month as he held them to a .207 average and walked away with the Eastern League Pitcher of the Month award for April.

Taylor posted a 4-0 mark and a 1.69 ERA in 3 relief appearances. In 21.1 innings, he had no saves, 17 strikeouts, and 5 walks.
