Smith Has Season End
Team officials for the <Kane County Cougars:team#257> confirmed that a torn flexor tendon in his elbow will force <Riley Wade Smith:player#21873> to miss the rest of the season. Smith sustained the injury on 06/15/2018. It remains to be seen who will replace Smith on the active roster.

Thus far this season <Smith:player#21873> has posted a 3-5 record, started 14 games, struck out 31 batters in 66.1 innings and compiled a 2.85 ERA.
