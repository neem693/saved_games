Fukuoka SoftBank's Despaigne Gets JPL Player of the Week 
<Alfredo Despaigne:player#40448> stepped up to the plate in a big way last week and today he took home the Pacific League Player of the Week honors.

The <Fukuoka SoftBank:team#412> first baseman ripped opposing pitchers for 13 hits in 27 at-bats and 5 home runs. He put together a sizzling .481 batting average as he knocked in 7 runs and scored 8 times.

So far in 2018 Despaigne is batting .268 with 8 home runs, 22 RBIs and 18 runs scored. He has 30 hits in 112 at-bats and a .366 OBP.
