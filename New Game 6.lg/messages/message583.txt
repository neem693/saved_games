Sugano Triumphs in JCL Player of the Week
<Tomoyuki Sugano:player#37332> is capable of a great game every time he takes the hill and last week the <Yomiuri Giants:team#422> hurler proved that point with gusto to become the newest Central League Player of the Week.

He picked up 2 wins and no losses and rang up 20 strikeouts in 18 innings to finish with a splendid 0.50 ERA.

For this year <Sugano:player#37332> has chalked up 3 wins and 2 losses in 6 starts with 46 strikeouts in 44 innings and a 2.45 ERA.
