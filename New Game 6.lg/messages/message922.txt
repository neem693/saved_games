Injured Sugiura Out 4-5 Months
The <Hokkaido Nippon-Ham Fighters:team#413> have made it official: <Toshihiro Sugiura:player#37669> is headed to the disabled list. The 26-year-old starting pitcher has been out since 05/18/2018 with an arthritic elbow. He is expected to miss 4-5 months.

<Sugiura:player#37669> has gone 1-4 this season with an ERA of 4.50 and 47 strikeouts in 50 innings pitched. Other clubs are hitting .270 against him.
