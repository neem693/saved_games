TFBL Awards Player of the Week
The baseball must have looked as big as a balloon to <Wen-Chieh Chen:player#39302> of <Taoyuan:team#464> last week. The center fielder had quite a 5-game stretch and today he was named the Taiwan Future Baseball League Player of the Week.

Chen delivered 9 hits in 19 at-bats to hit .474 with 2 home runs and 7 RBIs.

So far this season Chen is hitting .301 with 6 home runs, 18 RBIs and 21 runs scored. He has 31 hits in 103 at-bats and a .360 OBP.
