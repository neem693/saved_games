Suchy Takes FSL's Best Bat Trophy
<Michael Suchy:player#86> may be much younger than other hitters in the Florida State League, but he performed like a seasoned veteran in June to win the Batter of the Month award.

The 25-year-old <Bradenton:team#228> right fielder hit .416 with 5 home runs, 15 RBIs and 14 runs scored.

Batting .322 to date, Suchy has 13 home runs and 42 RBIs.
