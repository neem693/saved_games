Ellis Picked as April's Pitcher of the Month
There's something about a young rookie winning the American Conference Pitcher of the Month trophy that sends shock waves through the league. Such is the case with <Chris Ellis:player#14>, a 25-year-old starter for the <Memphis Redbirds:team#172>.

In April, he topped off the month with a 3-1 mark and 0.87 ERA in 5 starts, tossed 31 innings, fanned 22 and held opposing teams to a .196 average.
