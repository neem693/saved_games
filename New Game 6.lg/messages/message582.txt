Week's Premier Performer Honored by JPL
So far in his young career, <Shogo Nakamura:player#42697> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Pacific League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 25-year-old <Chiba Lotte Marines:team#411> shortstop hit at a torrid .524 clip as he collected 11 hits in 21 at-bats, 1 home run, 2 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

Nakamura is hitting .303 for the season with 4 home runs, 16 RBIs and 8 runs scored. He has 30 hits in 99 at-bats and played in 25 games.
