Star of the Week Picked in TFBL
According to the voters, <Austin Wilson:player#34158> of the <Taoyuan Elephants:team#464> had the best week of all Taiwan Future Baseball League hitters as he was named this week's Player of the Week.

The right fielder's accomplishments include 6 hits in 11 at-bats, a .545 batting average, 3 home runs, 4 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

Thus far this season Wilson is hitting .290 with 4 home runs, 8 RBIs and 11 runs scored. He has 9 hits in 31 at-bats and has played in 8 games.
