CAL Names JetHawks Ramos Winner
It was announced today that <Lancaster:team#233> first baseman <Roberto A. Ramos:player#41964> had received the California League Player of the Week Award for his impressive play last week.

All week long he was terrific at the plate and won the honor with a .421 BA (8-for-19), 3 home runs and 7 RBIs.

This season <Ramos:player#41964> is hitting .279 with 7 home runs.
