Early Season End for Loree
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the <Fubon Guardians:team#461>: starting pitcher <Mike Loree:player#39655> is out for the year. Loree suffered a torn ulnar collateral ligament on 04/05/2018. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.
