PC #1 Pitcher in May is Sacramento's Gage
<Matt Gage:player#42386> took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the Pacific Conference Pitcher of the Month award for May.

The 25-year-old <River Cats:team#185> starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 4-0 won-lost record in 6 starts. This month he fanned 23 batters in 40.2 innings and compiled a 1.77 ERA.

In 11 starts this season, <Gage:player#42386> has picked up 6 wins while suffering no losses. He has pitched 74 innings, allowed 59 hits, struck out 46 and posted a 1.70 ERA.
