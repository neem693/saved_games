Farrar Learns More about Injury
The <Beloit Snappers:team#253> have made it official: <Logan Farrar:player#44808> is headed to the disabled list. The 22-year-old right fielder has been out since 04/11/2018 with a torn labrum. He is expected to miss 4-5 months.

So far this year in 6 games Farrar is hitting .353 with no home runs, 0 RBIs and 4 runs scored.
