Marines Hurler Named EL's Best Starter
There's something about a young rookie winning the Eastern League Pitcher of the Month trophy that sends shock waves through the league. Such is the case with <Chihaya Sasaki:player#44072>, a 23-year-old starter for the <Chiba Lotte (Ni-Gun) Marines:team#423>.

In May, he topped off the month with a 5-0 mark and 1.59 ERA in 5 starts, tossed 34 innings, fanned 31 and held opposing teams to a .168 average.

To date this year <Sasaki:player#44072> has compiled a 5-1 mark with a 1.97 ERA in 7 starts.
