KBO Names Heroes Park Winner
When it came to hitting the baseball, <Byung-ho Park:player#38538> had few peers in the Korean Baseball Organization this past week.

The talented first baseman for Nexen tagged opposing pitchers for a .440 average and was voted the Player of the Week Award. Included in his stats were 11 hits in 25 at-bats, 4 homers, 6 runs scored and 8 RBIs.
