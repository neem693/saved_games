McKinstry Honored by California League
23-year-old <Zach McKinstry:player#21728> made a few <Rancho Cucamonga:team#226> headlines during this past week as the rookie third baseman proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and California League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 10 hits in 22 at-bats to put up a .455 average. McKinstry collected 1 home run, 8 RBIs and scored 4 runs.

Presently <McKinstry:player#21728> is batting .294 and has collected 1 home run, 22 RBIs, .338 OBP and scored 20 runs.
