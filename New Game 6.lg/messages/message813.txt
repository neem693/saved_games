Sangdong Loses Kim for Season
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the <Sangdong Giants:team#454>: reliever <Hyun Kim:player#45918> is out for the year. Kim suffered a ruptured finger tendon on 05/10/2018. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.
