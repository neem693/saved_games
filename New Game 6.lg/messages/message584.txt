KBO Player of the Week Goes to Lee of Lotte
<Dae-ho Lee:player#37555> is respected by most <Lotte Giants:team#443> fans for his no-frills style of play. The 35-year-old first baseman went about his business with vigor last week to grab the Korean Baseball Organization Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .577 average (15-for-26) with 1 home run, 12 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

This year <Lee:player#37555> is hitting at a .319 pace with 5 home runs and 27 RBIs. He has played in 31 games and scored 18 times.
