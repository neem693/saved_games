Central League Names Top Player
A battle-tested <Hisayoshi Chono:player#37313> declared war on opposing pitchers last week, attacking the ball like the well-schooled veteran he is. Today the 33-year-old <Yomiuri:team#422> right fielder collected the Central League Player of the Week Award.

He wrapped up the week with a .526 average, 10 hits in 19 at-bats, 1 home run, 6 RBIs and 2 runs scored.

On the year <Chono:player#37313> has hit .404 with 2 home runs, 9 RBIs and scored 6 runs.
