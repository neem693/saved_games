Dobayashi Selected Top Performer in JCL
When <Shota Dobayashi:player#37021> stepped into the batter's box, he meant business. His outstanding play this week has netted the <Hiroshima Toyo:team#419> third baseman the well-deserved Central League Player of the Week Award.

Playing in 6 games he lashed out a .550 average with 11 hits, 5 home runs, 11 RBIs and 9 runs scored.
