Great April Gets Lin CPBL Best Hitter Award
<Yi-Chuan Lin:player#39213> of the <Fubon Guardians:team#461> certainly made a resounding impression on the Chinese Professional Baseball League pitching corps in April and today reaped the reward as the league's best batter for the period.

The first baseman put up some good offensive figures as he hit .440 with 6 home runs, 23 RBIs and 20 runs scored.
