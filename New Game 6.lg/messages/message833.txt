WL Star of the Week Announced
Western League pitchers will attest that it was tough to get <Ariel Martinez:player#43048> of the <Chunichi (Ni-Gun) Dragons:team#429> out last week. His all-around performance at the plate made him the league's most recent Player of the Week.

The 21-year-old catcher boasted a .550 average with no home runs, 7 RBIs and 3 runs scored. <Martinez:player#43048> went 11-for-20 for the week.

Martinez is hitting .244 so far this season with 26 RBIs and 10 runs scored. He has 33 hits including 4 home runs, 19 walks and .342 OBP.
