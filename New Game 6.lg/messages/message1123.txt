Hiroshima Toyo Loses Johnson for Season
The <Hiroshima Toyo Carp:team#419> have lost the services of <Kris Johnson:player#22172> for the rest of this season. He suffered a torn ulnar collateral ligament in a recent ballgame and the follow-up report from the medical staff is not good. Johnson is out for the year.

This year <Johnson:player#22172> has registered 4 wins and 3 losses in 11 starts with a 3.77 ERA while registering 50 strikeouts and 12 walks in 62 innings.
