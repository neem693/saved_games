Faria Takes AL Top Starter Trophy
Over the past month <Jacob Faria:player#31500> has turned quite a few heads. He's young, talented and able to pitch with the established arms in the American League. He proved all that and now he's been named April's Pitcher of the Month.

The rookie for the <Tampa Bay Rays:team#57> posted 4-0 mark and a 1.29 ERA. In 5 starts he tossed 28 innings, permitted 21 hits and fanned 33 batters.
