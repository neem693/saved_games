Blue Wahoos Daal Snags SL Player of the Week
24-year-old <Calten Daal:player#36124> made a few <Pensacola:team#200> headlines during this past week as the rookie shortstop proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Southern League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 12 hits in 29 at-bats to put up a .414 average. Daal collected 1 home run, 7 RBIs and scored 7 runs.
