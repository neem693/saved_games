Eagan Earns SAL Player of the Week
<Clark Eagan:player#20626> went on a week-long hitting spree against South Atlantic League pitchers and captured the Player of the Week honors.

The <West Virginia:team#263> center fielder battered the opposition for 13 hits, no home runs, 5 RBI and scored 5 runs. He hit .448 and posted a .469 on-base percentage.
