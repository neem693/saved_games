Week's #1 Player: KIA's Choi
A battle-tested <Hyoung-woo Choi:player#38357> declared war on opposing pitchers last week, attacking the ball like the well-schooled veteran he is. Today the 34-year-old <KIA:team#445> left fielder collected the Korean Baseball Organization Player of the Week Award.

He wrapped up the week with a .565 average, 13 hits in 23 at-bats, 3 home runs, 9 RBIs and 8 runs scored.

This season <Choi:player#38357> has hit at a .360 clip and produced 35 runs batted in, 6 home runs and 40 runs.
