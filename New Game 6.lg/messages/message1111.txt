Wilson Crowned Batter of the Month in May
<Austin Wilson:player#34158> said he hopes everyone remembers his performance in May if he ends up struggling this year. His performance certainly was memorable, as the 26-year-old <Taoyuan Elephants:team#464> right fielder was a step ahead all other Taiwan Future Baseball League hitters and was named Batter of the Month.

On the month, he hit .308 with 5 home runs, 12 RBIs and 13 runs scored.
