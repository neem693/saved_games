Gavin Collins Winner in Carolina League
<Gavin Collins:player#27427> may be much younger than other hitters in the Carolina League, but he performed like a seasoned veteran in April to win the Batter of the Month award.

The 22-year-old <Lynchburg:team#245> third baseman hit .295 with 3 home runs, 14 RBIs and 20 runs scored.
