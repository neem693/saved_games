KFL's Top Hitter Announced
<Dong-wook Jung:player#45936> of the <Hwaseong Heroes:team#455> had a field day with Korean Futures League pitchers last month and was crowned the Batter of the Month as a result.

During June he compiled a .471 batting average, drove in 17 runs and scored 18 times. He also managed to pick up 7 walks to post a .519 on-base percentage.

This season <Jung:player#45936> is batting at a .348 clip with 10 home runs and 41 RBIs.
