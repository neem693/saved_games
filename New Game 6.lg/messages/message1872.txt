Injury Brings Season to End for Bird
<Kyle Bird:player#42831> expressed disappointment but also relief after finding out he would be out 11 months with a partially torn ulnar collateral ligament he suffered on 07/04/2018. Speaking to a reporter at the team's clubhouse, Bird, the <Montgomery:team#207> reliever said, "I'd rather know than not know. I'll just take it one day at a time."

This year <Bird:player#42831> has made 23 relief appearances with 5 saves. In 25.1 innings, he has fanned 23 hitters and recorded 2 wins and 2 losses with a 1.78 ERA.
