SL's #1 Batter Chosen
<Chattanooga Lookouts:team#208> fans believe they've got a talented young star in <Brent Rooker:player#43953>.

He did nothing to dissuade that notion in June, as he hit .324 with 8 home runs, 25 RBIs and 20 runs scored.

The end result was the left fielder being named the Southern League Batter of the Month.

Rooker is hitting .324 so far this season with 25 RBIs and 20 runs scored. He has 33 hits including 8 home runs, 7 walks and .386 OBP.
