Higurashi Earns WL Player of the Week
So far in his young career, <Yamato Higurashi:player#46080> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Western League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 19-year-old <Fukuoka SoftBank (Ni-Gun) Hawks:team#430> right fielder hit at a torrid .500 clip as he collected 10 hits in 20 at-bats, 2 home runs, 8 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

So far this year <Higurashi:player#46080> is batting .345 with 12 home runs.
