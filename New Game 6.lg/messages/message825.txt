SL Awards Player of the Week Trophy
A hot-hitting week got <Alex Jackson:player#1155> the Southern League Player of the Week trophy. He really wore out his welcome with league pitchers.

The <Mississippi:team#205> right fielder slammed them for a .440 average, 11 hits in 25 at-bats, 3 home runs, 10 RBIs and scored 5 runs.

In the postgame press conference, <Jackson:player#1155> shared his hitting philosophy with the BNN scribes: "Just look for the seams and hit between them... nothin' more than that."

Thus far this season Jackson is hitting .248 with 7 home runs, 21 RBIs and 16 runs scored. He has 32 hits in 129 at-bats and has played in 32 games.
